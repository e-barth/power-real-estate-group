Your Roadmap to Buying a Home

Follow these 8 simple steps to move from search to closing with confidence.

  • Step 1

    Establish Your Budget

    Before falling in love with a home, make sure you’re in love with the payment. We’ll help you define your ideal monthly payment, review obligations, and clarify loan/rate options.

    • Define your ideal monthly payment based on comfort and goals
    • Review current financial obligations
    • Determine loan qualification & rate options

    Calculator tip: Use the Affordability & Mortgage tools to model “what-ifs.”

  • Step 2

    Find Your Future Home

    We’ll match properties to your lifestyle and priorities — so your search stays focused.

    • Preferred locations & neighborhoods
    • Bedrooms, bathrooms & home type (SFR or condo)
    • Amenities & school districts

    Not sure how far your budget goes in a certain area? Quick check with the Affordability Calculator.

  • Step 3

    Write a Winning Offer

    When you find “the one,” we craft a strategy that stands out without overpaying.

    • Days on market & competition
    • Seller motivation & timing
    • Comparable sales & pricing strategy

    Every detail matters — and we’ll make sure your offer is competitive and compelling.

  • Step 4

    Enter Escrow

    Escrow is a neutral third party that manages funds and compliance once your offer is accepted.

    • Earnest money deposit due within three business days
    • Loan process officially begins
    • Inspections & due diligence kick off

    Calculator tip: Re-check payments as rates/credits change with the Mortgage Calculator.

  • Step 5

    Discovery & Inspections

    During the inspection window (typically 12–17 days in CA), we make sure there are no surprises.

    • Schedule a professional home inspection
    • Review seller disclosures & insurance eligibility
    • Keep loan approval on track

    Goal: clarity & confidence before you commit.

  • Step 6

    Contingencies

    Contingencies protect you if unexpected issues come up. If the property doesn’t meet expectations, we can renegotiate — or walk away.

    • Inspection results (roof, plumbing, termite, etc.)
    • Seller disclosures (condition, history, repairs)
    • Loan approval & insurance eligibility

  • Step 7

    Negotiations

    We negotiate in your best interest so you get the value and terms you deserve.

    • Repair requests & home warranties
    • Closing cost credits
    • Items included with the sale

    Calculator tip: See how repair credits or price changes affect payments using the Mortgage Calculator.

  • Step 8

    Final Walk-Through & Closing

    We’ll confirm repairs, set up essentials, and hand you the keys.

    • Final walk-through to verify agreed repairs
    • Utility setup & mail forwarding
    • Review final docs & receive keys on closing day
    Planning upgrades after move-in? Pressure-test your budget with the calculators above.
